How we collect, use, and store data.

Privacy Policy

1. Who we are

Prasis is a student-built front-end that connects to the Noroff Auction API (v2). This site is for educational purposes.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: name, @stud.noroff.no email, avatar URL.
  • Usage data: pages visited, basic analytics (non-identifying).
  • Listing and bidding data you create in the app.

3. How we use data

  • Authenticate you and show your profile and credits.
  • Create, display, and manage listings and bids.
  • Improve the application during the course project.

4. Cookies & storage

  • We store an auth token and profile info in localStorage to keep you signed in.
  • We may use cookies for session or basic analytics.

5. Third parties

API requests are made to the official Noroff v2 API. Media you upload may be hosted by Noroff’s infrastructure as defined by that API.

6. Data retention

Account, listing, and bid data are kept as long as your account exists or as needed for the course. You can request removal during the project period.

7. Your rights

  • Access your data (profile, listings, bids).
  • Update your avatar and account display data.
  • Request deletion of locally stored auth data (you can also log out).

8. Security

We use the API’s authentication and store tokens in the browser. Do not share your credentials.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Use our Contact page.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
